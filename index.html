Unlocking Billion-Dollar Value from Agricultural Waste

AG Intellisense is a deep-tech R&D company building advanced manufacturing methods in the green chemistry space — commercialising established science that academic research has proven but industry has failed to act on.

We take food waste primarily from agricultural streams and transform it into valuable new products — starting with food-grade cellulose and nanocellulose, two of the fastest-growing advanced materials globally, with applications across packaging, composites, biomedical devices and coatings.

We are actively raising capital to move from proven lab-scale development to commercial manufacturing in Far North Queensland.

Waste Metabolomics

We use untargeted metabolomics (HPLC, LC-MS, GC-MS) to map the full chemical profile of agricultural waste streams before extraction — ensuring every process is data-driven, efficient, and high-yield.

Green Extraction

Solvent-minimal, food-grade processes developed in partnership with leading Australian universities. We preserve compound quality and deliver purity standards that meet commercial and regulatory requirements.

Scalable Commercial Impact

Every tonne processed reduces landfill and burning, displaces synthetic materials, and creates regional jobs. Our model is built for rapid scale-up and attractive investor returns in the multi-billion-dollar bio-materials market.

Our Driving Principles

Our principles shape how we develop processes, build products, and create lasting environmental and commercial value from agricultural waste.

Scientific Rigour

We use advanced metabolomics — including HPLC, LC-MS, GC-MS, and untargeted profiling — to characterise every waste stream at the molecular level before development begins. This analytical foundation ensures our extraction processes are built on real chemistry and deliver consistent, high-purity results.

Circular Economy

Agricultural waste is a resource, not a liability. We take what would otherwise be discarded from sugarcane mills, banana farms, and coffee processors and transform it into materials that re-enter the supply chain at significantly higher value. Circularity isn’t something we apply after the fact — it’s the starting point.

Impact Through Commercialisation & Investment Readiness

The fastest way to reduce agricultural waste at scale is to make it commercially valuable. Every AG Intellisense project is engineered for market success and manufacturing scale. We own the IP, control the process, and are structured for investment — delivering both environmental returns and strong financial upside for partners who join us now.
