The Future of Crop Diversification in FNQ

Far North Queensland (FNQ) is a region brimming with opportunities for agricultural innovation. With its tropical climate, diverse ecosystems, and a history rooted in farming, FNQ is poised to lead the way in sustainable and diversified agriculture. However, the region's dependence on monocultures presents significant challenges, including soil degradation, economic vulnerability, and limited sustainability. As the agricultural landscape evolves, crop diversification emerges as a vital strategy for the future of FNQ.
