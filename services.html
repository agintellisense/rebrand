Untarget Plant Metabolomics for product development.

Untargeted plant metabolomics is a comprehensive analytical approach that aims to profile all detectable metabolites in a biological sample without any prior selection of specific compounds. This is crucial for product development in plants as it enables the discovery of novel bioactive compounds. Ag-Intellisense utilizes GCMS-QP2010 for untargeted plant metabolomics, enabling comprehensive profiling of plant metabolites to uncover bioactive compounds with potential for product development.

Plant Metabolomics Data Interpretation & Pathway Analysis

At Ag-Intellisense, we specialize in GCMS-QP2010-based plant metabolomics analysis, utilizing MetaboAnalyst for advanced statistical interpretation, including PCA, PLS-DA, volcano plots, and pathway analysis. We help biotechnology and pharmaceutical companies, AgTech startups, and research institutions uncover metabolic changes, identify bioactive compounds, and explore functional pathways for research and product development.
